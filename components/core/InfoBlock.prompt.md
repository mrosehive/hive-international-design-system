Titled panel with a 3px left-accent rule — for guidance, callouts, capability summaries, and compliance notes.

```jsx
<InfoBlock title="Logo Color Rule" accent="gold">
  The primary logo uses Hive Gold and Charcoal on a transparent background.
</InfoBlock>
```

`accent="gold"` (default) for emphasis; `accent="silver"` for quieter supporting notes. Title is optional and renders in Bebas.
