Initials (or image) avatar on a charcoal disc with a hairline ring — for team members, authors, and contacts.

```jsx
<Avatar name="Dana Reyes" size={44} />
<Avatar name="Marcus Hale" src="/team/hale.jpg" ring="gold" />
```

Initials are derived from `name` (first two words). `ring="gold"` for active/emphasis. `size` is the px diameter.
