Compact status/category pill in the low-saturation Hive palette — use for statuses, classifications, and counts.

```jsx
<Badge tone="accent">Active Contract</Badge>
<Badge tone="success" solid>Cleared</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral` (default, silver), `accent` (gold — emphasis, keep scarce), `success`, `danger`, `info`. `solid` fills the pill instead of tinting it. Stays uppercase and tracked.
